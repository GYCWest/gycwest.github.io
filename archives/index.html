2020 — 20/20 Vision, Purpose refocused

GYC West Yearly Main Event

God has for each of us a clear vision, an elevated purpose. He wants those who will "press towards the mark for the prize of the high calling of God in Christ Jesus." Today, as young adults, our vision can be set! Our purpose can be firm! But is our purpose firm in Christ? Is our vision clear? And what is our purpose? How can we live to glorify God in the 21st century?

Do you want 20/20 vision? Come join us this year at Barlow Group Camp where we will have our purpose refocused and our vision set on higher things.

Location: Barlow Group Camp, CA.

Text: "Forgetting those things which are behind, I press toward the mark for the prize of the high calling of God in Christ Jesus." — Philippians 3:13, 14

2019 — Let These Stand

GYC West Yearly Main Event

God has called every generation to stand unflinchingly for Christ and His truth, and today is no different. Are you ready to stand though the heavens fall? Come aside with GYC West this summer for an unforgettable, outdoor experience down by the riverside at the Albion Field Station to prepare to stand in these last days for the final conflict ahead.

Location: Albion, CA.

Text: "Wherefore take unto you the whole armour of God, that ye may be able to withstand in the evil day, and having done all, to stand." — Ephesians 6:13 (KJV)
