AUG 15-17

Please note the following conditions:

  • Registrants age 15 or younger must have a paid chaperon.
  • Submitting registration implies consent to be recorded.
  • Registrations submitted within a week of the event are not guaranteed any meals. Please register ASAP to reserve your meals!

Other pricing notes:

  • Registration price adjusts according to age as you register.
  • Registration for children ages 0-6 is free; registration for ages 7-13 is half-price.
  • Sabbath registration is free, but required for entry. Sabbath lunch can be purchased during registration.
